Each filter, depending on the product purchased, differs in the filtration mixture. The best quality as well as the degree of filtration can boast the Pro filter, it is designed for severely hard water. Standard filters are designed for water medium-hard

HOW DO HAYSEN & CLARVIS FILTERS WORK?

  1. Filter mesh - Holds larger contaminants such as rust, sand, sediment.
  2. Ion exchange resin - removes excess magnesium and calcium ions, thereby leveling water hardness. Activated carbon impregnated with silver - in the process of absorption and catalytic reduction removes pesticides, herbicides, chlorine, detergents. Perceptibly improves the smell and taste of water.
  3. Filter grid - Traps contaminants inside the filter.
